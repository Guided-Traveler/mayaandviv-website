Who we are

Hi, we're Maya & Viv.

Maya & Viv Adventures is a well-established tour company based in San Pedro, Belize — owned and run by a mother and daughter with more than nine years of guiding experience across the Belize Barrier Reef and deep into the mainland jungle.

We built this company around three things we grew up valuing ourselves: the comfort of our guests, the safety of every trip, and the kind of personal attention you only get from a family-run operation. No middlemen. No scripted tour chatter. Just us, the reef, the ruins, and you.

Whether you're a thrill-seeker searching for adrenaline-pumping activities like exploring the mysterious temples of the Maya, ziplining through the treetop canopies of our jungle, or you're yearning to immerse yourself in the renowned waters of Belize — we'd love to show you our island and our country.

— Maya & Viv

What guides us

Three things we refuse to compromise on.

Local, always.

Both of us were born and raised here. Every story you hear on tour is ours to tell — not read from a script.

Safety first.

Certified gear, licensed guides, up-to-date boats and vans. We won't run a trip we wouldn't take our own family on.

Small groups, real attention.

We cap every tour deliberately small. You'll actually know your guide's name — and they'll know yours.
