Tours & Excursions

Two ways to experience Belize.

Reef at sunrise, ruins in the afternoon. Pick a day, or string a few together — we'll build the trip around you.

Category 01

Snorkeling Excursions

Some of the clearest water in the Caribbean, right off our pier. From Hol Chan Marine Reserve to the outer cayes, these are slow-paced, small-group trips with real time in the water — not a drive-by.

Half Day From$85

Hol Chan & Shark Ray Alley

Drift through the narrow cut at Hol Chan Marine Reserve, then float alongside nurse sharks and southern stingrays at the famous sandy shallows just beyond the reef.

Mainland Excursions

A short ferry, a scenic drive, and you're inside a different country. Ancient Mayan temples rising out of rainforest, underground rivers lit only by headlamp, zip lines strung across the jungle canopy.

Full Day From$165

Xunantunich Temple Tour

Cross to the mainland and climb the limestone steps of El Castillo. Howler monkeys in the canopy, the Mopan River hand-cranked ferry, and a Belizean lunch on the way home.

~9 hours Guide + transport Lunch included
